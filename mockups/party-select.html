Gemma's Take

Three paths, three tradeoffs. IU Kelley is your safest bet on cost — you WIN Fight Student Loans and keep Burnout in check, but AI exposure is real and there's no ceiling-breaking power.

UIUC splits the difference on cost and gives you AI protection, but you're stuck under the same ceiling. The creative paths it opens — like Creative Director — also start from Michigan, so the branch overlap is real.

Michigan is the swing-for-the-fences pick — highest earnings, breaks through the ceiling, AI-proof — but the debt is more than double IU's, and burnout risk is real. It's the only build that opens the CMO path.

The question isn't which is best. It's which risk you'd rather carry.
