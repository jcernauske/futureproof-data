Gemma Interactions

GemmaThinking

The canonical inline status. Spinner + attribution text with gap-3. Text is font-body text-small text-text-secondary. Convention: third-person present continuous — "Gemma is …ing …".

Gemma is matching your input…
Gemma is writing your action plan…
Gemma is analyzing your build…

Attribution Line

Appears at the top of any Gemma-produced card. Static star, muted label, raw user input bold in text-secondary font-semibold. Past tense — "Gemma matched…", "Gemma found…".

Gemma matched “pre-med”

Match Card

The resolved-match container. Match title is text-accent-insight — Gemma's voice, audible in content. Career preview items render in text-accent-info and brighten to text-primary on hover. Low-confidence → caution. On confirm → brief thrive flash (320ms) before handoff.

Tone Exploration

Same card, six accents. Title, attribution input, left stripe, and section label all inherit a single --tone variable. Careers stay neutral so the comparison isolates the tone-carrying elements. Breathing glow swapped for a static tinted shadow — judge color, not motion.
